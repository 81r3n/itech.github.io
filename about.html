About Us

Who We Are

iTechnologies is a digital transformation company that provides full scale IT consulting, software development and deployment services. Established in 2008, iTechnologies endeavors to bridge the gap between supply and demand for ICT services and software systems, and develop the local IT competency to provide the Bhutanese market with the necessary IT services. We operate with the mission to provide IT and ITES services with priority based on delivery and customer/client satisfaction and quality post development/deployment/implementation support.

Our Team

We are a team of 100% bhutanese software developers. We have teams specialized in Open Source Technology Stack (PHP frameworks and J2EE frameworks) and Microsoft Technology Stack (Dot Net Framework). Adequate skills are available in majority of the commonly used backend databases (MySQL, Postgresql, MariaDB, and MS SQL Server).Our team has been involved in development of several software applications for government agencies in Bhutan. We have also worked with international development partners to execute software applications development for government agencies are end clients.
